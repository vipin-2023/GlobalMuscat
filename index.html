What We Do

BUILDING INFORMATION MODELLING

3D Model developments on LOD 300,LOD 350,LOD 400 & LOD 500 at AEC industry.

2D CAD AND CAD COMPLIANCE

CAD drawings and CAD compliance incorporating facility management data to CAD on Architecture /Structure /MEP Services

STAFF AUGEMENTATION

Staff augmentation and recruitment services for experienced and highly motivated professionals for our clients.

CLASH DETECTION AND AVOIDANCE

3D models to coordinate all disciplines (e.g. structural and mechanical) and to identify/resolve possible clashes between virtual elements prior to actual construction or fabrication.

4D CONSTRUCTION SIMULATION

Incorporate the time schedule obtained from Project Management in Primavera/MS Project or excel to BIM elements for Construction Simulation.

POINT SCAN CLOUD

Transform Point Clouds into 3D objects using specialized tools to generate As-Built Models or model parts.
